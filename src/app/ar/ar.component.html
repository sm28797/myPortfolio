FDM

FDM - Furniture Dimension Managing

While making the online shopping of the furniture the major problem that the user face is that whether the furniture they like or choose will fir in their home in the available space or not. This makes the online furniture shopping a disaster for the users. The users need to measure each and every dimension of the space available in their home and then match them to each and every furniture available at the online furniture store.

Sometimes the users purchase the furniture and then when the furniture arrives at their doorstep, they realize that it is just not the size they wanted to purchase. This leads to a big failure in the online furniture shopping. The application provides the virtual placing of furniture in the real world so as to signify whether the furniture can be placed in the available space at the house or not.

This tool or application will allow a better user experience by allowing them to view the furniture easily just by sitting at their home and just taking a view of the furniture in their houses. The application can be integrated with the various furniture vendors in the city in order that they can register their stores on the application and provide their furniture models to the user through this application. These vendors can provide the different furniture items available in their store on the application so that the users can view them and contact the store in order to buy the furniture of their choice.
